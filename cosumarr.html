Cosumar

Cosumar ou Compagnie Sucrerie Marocaine et de Raffinage est un groupe marocain spécialisé dans l'extraction, le raffinage et le conditionnement du sucre. La Cosumar produit du sucre à partir de la canne et de la betterave à sucre cultivée dans les régions marocaines et procède au raffinage du sucre brut importé. Le groupe bénéficie d'une subvention de l'État Marocain via la caisse de compensation afin de maintenir un faible niveau des prix du sucre. Selon les statistiques de la Cosumar, la consommation par habitant et par an est estimée à 37 kg soit 101 g/jour1. Les principaux actionnaires de la Cosumar sont Wilmar International (30%), la CIMR (15,2%) et Wafa Assurance (7,1%).

COSUMAR HISTORY

COSUMAR’s history has been inseparable with the early industrialization of white sugar in Morocco. Formerly of homemade fabrication, it was in 1929 that the first sugar refinery was established in the Roches Noires district of Casablanca. Let’s discover the milestones in the history of this major Moroccan company.

1929

BIRTH OF COSUMA
Creation of the Casablanca refinery’s historic site by the Société Nouvelle des Raffineries de Sucre Saint-Louis in MARSEILLE. 100 tonnes of sugar were produced per day, exclusively in the form of sugarloaves.

1967

FROM COSUMA TO COSUMAR
The Moroccan State acquires 50% of the capital.

1985

COSUMAR: SUBSIDIARY OF THE FIRST PRIVATE GROUP, ONA
ONA group takes over Cosumar’s capital, now listed in the Casablanca stock exchange.

1993

ACQUISITION OF THE DOUKKALAS SUGAR FACTORIES
Cosumar absorbs the Doukkala sugar factories (Zemamra and Sidi Bennour), of which it already held a significant share.

2005

BIRTH OF A GROUP
Acquisition of 4 public sugar companies: SUTA, SURAC, SUNABEL and SUCRAFOR

2006

THE FIRST UPGRADING INVESTMENTS
Project for the extension of the beet processing capacity to 15 000 tonnes of beet/day at the Sidi Bennour sugar factory, amount of investment: MDH 850.

2009

COSUMAR’S 80 YEARS AND RECOGNITION AS AN AGGREGATOR MODEL OF THE SUGAR INDUSTRY
Barid Al Maghrib dedicates a stamp to the Moroccan sugar industry on the occasion of the 80th anniversary of its inception. COSUMAR is awarded FAO’s medal for its aggregation model.

2012

CGEM’S LABEL AND « CSR PIONEER» AWARD
COSUMAR is awarded CGEM’s social responsibility label and designated as a Pioneer of CSR in Africa by the CSR Institute

2013

ENTRY OF WILMAR INTO THE GROUP’S CAPITAL
SNI transfers 27.5% of COSUMAR’s capital to Singapore’s Wilmar group.

2014

SALE BY SNI OF A MAJORITY STAKE IN COSUMAR
Entry of a consortium of institutional investors into the capital of the company: Axa Assurances, CNIA Saada, RMA Watanya, SCR, Wafa Assurances, MAMDA, MCMA, CDG, CMR, RCAR, Wafa Gestion, and CFG.

2015

NEW BRAND IDENTITY
COSUMAR has announced its new Brand Identity during the INTERNATIONAL AGRICULTURE SHOW IN MOROCCO 2015.
