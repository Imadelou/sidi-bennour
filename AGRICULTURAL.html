AGRICULTURAL IN SIDI BENNOURE

❮ ❯

Agriculture
The share of the province of Sidi Bennour represents 40% of the vegetable productive value, with 2,834 MDH, of which the beet sector takes the lion's share. As such, Sidi Bennour is home to the largest sugar factory in Morocco, whose production is close to 15,000 tons per day. In addition, the province represents 18% of the animal productive value with 1,809 MDH.
